# Instagram Hashtag Strategy

## Hashtag Tiers

| Tier | Post Volume | Purpose | Count |
|------|------------|---------|-------|
| Mega | 1M+ posts | Broad reach, high competition | 2-3 |
| Mid | 100K-1M | Target audience, moderate competition | 5-7 |
| Niche | 10K-100K | Specific community, low competition | 5-7 |
| Brand | Any | Brand identity, tracking | 1-3 |

## Optimal Count

- **Feed**: 15-30 hashtags (Instagram allows max 30)
- **Reel**: 15-20 hashtags (fewer, more targeted)
- **Story**: 5-10 hashtags (sticker format)
- **First comment strategy**: Put 10-15 additional hashtags in first comment

## Placement Strategy

### Option A: Caption Bottom (Default)
Place hashtags at bottom of caption, separated by dot spacers:

```
{caption}

・
・
・

#tag1 #tag2 #tag3
```

### Option B: First Comment (Recommended for Engagement)
- Main caption: 5-8 most relevant hashtags blended into text
- First comment: 15-20 additional hashtags
- Benefits: Cleaner caption, same discoverability

## Generation Rules

1. **Research keywords**: Extract primary keywords from content/topic
2. **Map to tiers**: Select hashtags across all 4 tiers
3. **Check relevance**: Every hashtag must relate to post content
4. **Avoid banned tags**: Do not use Instagram-restricted hashtags
5. **Mix languages**: For bilingual accounts, mix ja + en tags
6. **Seasonal**: Add seasonal/trending tags when relevant
7. **No spaces in tags**: Use camelCase for multi-word English tags
8. **Japanese tags**: No spaces needed (Japanese characters are continuous)
